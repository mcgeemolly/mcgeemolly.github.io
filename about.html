Molly McGee

Solving problems and helping people

I'm passionate about solving problems and helping people, and my insatiable curiosity and love of learning mean I'm constantly finding new and exciting ways to accomplish both. I have experience working in product management, digital asset management (DAM) librarianship, instructional design and course development, graphic and web design, writing/editing, and making the perfect cup of coffee.

I graduated from the School of Information at the University of Texas at Austin in 2018 with my MSIS (Master of Science in Information Studies). During my time there, I was employed as a Teaching Assistant (TA) and IT Lab staff member, creating instructional technology content and project managing for our team of twelve other TAs. I currently work as a digital marketing product manager for Dell, serving as a liason between business and IT teams to enable development of new features, training, and feedback cycles.

My B.A. in English with minors in Business Administration and Communication from Texas A&M University in my hometown of College Station, Texas developed my skills at writing and communicating. In my spare time, I enjoy writing historical fiction, researching family genealogy, and learning new tools and techniques.
