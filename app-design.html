Mobile App Design

Research Paper

During my Information Architecture & Design course, my five-person team researched, planned, and developed a mobile application intended to help people find clothes that fit.

Research

We began by performing user and market research. I worked with two other team members to develop a competitive evaluation of ten other clothing apps that might be competitors. We also created and distributed a 21-question survey, which provided us with feedback from 3000 respondents.

We learned from the survey that the biggest pain-point people have with shopping and managing their clothing is finding clothes that fit properly. We began narrowing our focus and feature set, and I interviewed three users to gather more details about peopleâ€™s struggles with finding clothes that fit.

Design

While my other team members began designing low-fidelity wireframes using Adobe XD, I began designing high-fidelity mockups with Adobe Photoshop. I also took the lead on developing and organizing documentation.
