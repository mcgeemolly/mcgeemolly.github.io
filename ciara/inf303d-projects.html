Delivery Demand Affects Business Plans

My research project addressed how businesses have adapted their technology and practices to address the demand for delivery services during COVID-19. Through this project, I learned about the different innovations in delivery services for companies such as Amazon since the COVID-19 pandemic. Further, I was educated on how businesses are changing their practices to adapt to the increase in online business and improve safety precautions. Also, I learned what specific types of industries and businesses are profiting or losing business due to the increased demand for online and delivery services.
