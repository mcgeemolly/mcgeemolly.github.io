Bush Presidential Library | August - October 2015

Cold War Documentary Research

In order to gain experience working with archival materials, I worked with an independent screenwriter and producer, as well as archivists at the George H.W. Bush Presidential Archive to gather, digitize, and organize relevant documents for a documentary about the fall of the Soviet Union, based on the book The Last Empire by Serhii Plokhy.
