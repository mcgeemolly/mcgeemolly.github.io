Molly McGee

Solving problems and helping people

Hi there! I'm a graduate student pursuing my MSIS at the University of Texas at Austin School of Information with an interest in UX design, Archives, and instructional design. My favorite part about any work environment is learning about new ideas and technologies, and discovering how they can be used to create efficient and sustainable systems. I take great satisfaction in making order out of chaos.

I currently work at the School of Information as a Teaching Assistant (TA) in the IT Lab, creating instructional technology content, project managing for our team of twelve TAs, and aiding students and faculty with technology issues. I can also be found organizing marketing assets at Dell as a Digital Asset Management librarian, working with global teams to achieve maximum efficiency and findability.

My B.A. in English with minors in Business Administration and Communication from Texas A&M University in my hometown of College Station, Texas developed my skills at writing and communicating. In my spare time, I enjoy writing historical fiction, researching family genealogy, and learning new software.

Selected projects in my portfolio are listed in the Portfolio tab, and can be filtered by Management, Solutions, Design, and Research. For more detailed information, check out my resume.
