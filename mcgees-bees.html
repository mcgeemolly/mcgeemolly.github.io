McGees' Bees

❮ ❯

McGees' Bees cold process soap is made from natural ingredients and raw honey from bees living on the Old Republic Farm in Bryan, TX. Click here to email us and learn how you can order soap.

Different scents include:

  • Pure honey soap
  • Lavender honey soap
  • Cinnamon Swirl honey soap
  • Rosemary-lemon honey soap
  • Tea Tree honey soap
  • Citrus honey soap

All soaps are made from a combination of:

  • Water
  • Sodium hydroxide (lye)
  • Olive oil
  • Coconut oil
  • Vegetable shortning
  • Raw honey
  • Essential oils (Lavender, Rosemary-lemon, Tea Tree, Grapefruit)
  • Other add-ins (cinnamon powder added to cinnamon swirl soap, paprika added to Rosemary-lemon soap)
