At the IT Lab, I learned how to leverage screencapture and video editing tools like Camtasia to create tutorials on software, primarily focusing on open-source programs like GIMP and LibreOffice. I created scripts, filmed screencaptures, recorded audio, added special effects, and then uploaded the video to the iSchool Tutorials wiki, adding a timestamped transcription with Glifos.

This led to an opportunity to create instructional videos for Dell for the purpose of teaching users how to access and use a digital asset management system (DAMS). While these videos are for internal use only, feel free to browse my other software tutorials to see the products of my work.
